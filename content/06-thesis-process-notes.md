## V4 Structural Innovation: Personal Opening

The key breakthrough: 4 paragraphs of lived cultural memory BEFORE the thesis.

1. **Runescape → pseudonym pipeline** — Grand Exchange = price discovery, world 2 Varrock = information asymmetry, "trim your armor" = trust assumptions
2. **Poloniex trollbox** — ETH $1→$10, "WOW MONERO WOW DASH WOW UNLIMITED COCAINE" ban, DAO hack ETC listing, frozen withdrawals, trollbox shutdown June 2017
3. **BCH blocksize wars** — Roger Ver vs Core, Theymos "90% should leave", Craig Wright BBC claim, Andresen "bamboozled", BSV hash war
4. **Boryoku Dragonz / peak cycle** — 1,111 SOL legendary auction, YAM rebase bug, Chef Nomi $14M SUSHI dump, BAYC 0.08→152 ETH, Larry David FTX ad

Then: "And now Tether has roughly 300 employees. In 2024, it made $13 billion in profit." — the contrast is the whole point.

## Why This Works (Anti-AI Properties)

- Personal anecdotes are high Kolmogorov complexity — AI can't generate "WOW MONERO WOW DASH WOW UNLIMITED COCAINE" from a prompt
- Cultural details resist AI patterns: trollbox bans, Stellar bags, margin calls into empty order books
- The opening reads as someone sharing what they lived through, not presenting organized research
- DISORDER principle embodied: the memories feel like discovery, not presentation
