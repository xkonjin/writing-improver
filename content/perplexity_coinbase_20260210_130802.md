## Query 3
**Question**: Coinbase Super Bowl 60 ad Backstreet Boys Everybody karaoke commercial failure analysis - what mechanisms caused the hatred?

The Coinbase Super Bowl 60 ad featuring the Backstreet Boys' "Everybody" underperformed relative to competitor messaging, though the search results don't support characterizing it as universally "hated." Instead, the ad faced specific structural and reception challenges.

**Performance metrics reveal the core issue.** T-Mobile's competing Backstreet Boys spot ranked 17th out of 98 ads on EDO's TV Outcomes rankings (which measure immediate engagement predictive of future sales), while Coinbase's ranked 47th[1]. This significant gap suggests the ad failed to generate the immediate consumer response needed for advertising effectiveness.

**Creative reception was mixed and divided.** Some creative professionals praised the spot—Annika Welander, CEO of Someoddpilot, called it "creative that punches so far out of its weight class"[1]. However, others found it ineffective. Amir Sahba, founder of Thinkingbox, stated: "T-Mobile gave us something to feel. Coinbase gave us something confusing to figure out in real time."[1] This suggests the ad's minimalist, lo-fi karaoke aesthetic may have obscured rather than clarified its message.

**Contextual headwinds compounded the challenge.** Coinbase faced significant industry skepticism entering Super Bowl 60. Bitcoin had dropped 20% since the start of 2026, and the crypto sector was still recovering from FTX's 2022 collapse and founder Sam Bankman-Fried's imprisonment for fraud[1]. The ad attempted to position crypto as accessible and universal ("crypto is for everybody"), but this messaging may have felt tone-deaf amid broader market turmoil and regulatory uncertainty.

**The dual Backstreet Boys saturation may have diluted impact.** Both Coinbase and T-Mobile aired Backstreet Boys ads during the same quarter, creating viewer confusion about which brand owned which message[1]. This simultaneous deployment likely reduced the memorability and distinctiveness of Coinbase's spot.
