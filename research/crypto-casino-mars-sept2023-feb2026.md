### GENIUS Act — U.S. Stablecoin Regulation

**Timeline:**

- July 17, 2025: GENIUS Act became law
- July 18, 2025: President Trump signed legislation

**Implementation Schedule:**

- July 18, 2026: Regulators must promulgate final implementing regulations
- January 2027: GENIUS Act enforcement deadline
- Progress: FDIC Board approved notice of proposed rulemaking for application provisions

**Framework:**

- Regulates: Payment stablecoins (digital assets redeemable for fixed value)
- Permitted issuers:
  1. Subsidiary of insured depository institution
  2. Federal-qualified nonbank payment stablecoin issuer
  3. State-qualified payment stablecoin issuer

**Global Impact:**

- Created international benchmark
- Accelerated global momentum for stablecoin policy development

**Sources:** [Congress.gov GENIUS Act](https://www.congress.gov/bill/119th-congress/senate-bill/1582), [FDIC Press Release](https://www.fdic.gov/news/press-releases/2025/fdic-approves-proposal-establish-genius-act-application-procedures-fdic), [Latham & Watkins Analysis](https://www.lw.com/en/insights/the-genius-act-of-2025-stablecoin-legislation-adopted-in-the-us)

### MiCA — European Stablecoin Regulation

**Implementation Timeline:**

- June 30, 2024: Rules on asset-referenced tokens (ARTs) and e-money tokens (EMTs) enforced
- December 30, 2024: Full MiCA enforcement (Crypto Asset Service Providers provisions)
- January 2025: CASPs must begin applying for licenses
- Q1 2025: NCAs must ensure CASP compliance with non-MiCA compliant ARTs/EMTs
- Grandfathering: 18-month transition period for existing providers

**Stablecoin Requirements:**

- 1:1 ratio reserve in liquid assets
- Regular transparency reports
- Capital requirements
- Detailed whitepapers (Article 6)
- Mandatory regular audits of reserves

**Sources:** [Legal Nodes MiCA](https://www.legalnodes.com/article/mica-regulation-explained), [Norton Rose Fulbright MiCA](https://www.nortonrosefulbright.com/en/knowledge/publications/2cec201e/regulating-crypto-assets-in-europe-practical-guide-to-mica), [Walkers Global MiCA](https://www.walkersglobal.com/en/Insights/2025/01/The-EUs-crypto-regulation-MiCA-is-now-fully-applicable)

## CONCLUSION

**Matt Huang's "Casino on Mars" thesis validation:**

The data shows a clear two-phase pattern:

1. **2023-2024:** Speculation dominated (memecoins, NFTs, airdrop farming) — massive value creation and destruction, peak farming activity
2. **2024-2026:** Infrastructure emerged (stablecoin payments, ETF rails, L2 scale, TradFi integration) — regulatory clarity unlocked institutional capital

**Key evidence speculation bootstrapped infrastructure:**

- Solana's memecoin activity drove developer adoption (83% increase, first to surpass Ethereum since 2016)
- Airdrop farming (7M+ addresses) trained users on L2s, created liquidity for later utility
- NFT boom/bust funded wallet innovation (account abstraction, gasless transactions)
- Speculation-driven fees ($603M Solana, $514M Ethereum) funded network upgrades (Dencun, L2 scaling)

**Key evidence of decoupling:**

- Stablecoins now 30% of on-chain volume (utility) vs. memecoins declining to $53B from $150B peak
- Real yield protocols: Revenue sharing tripled (5% → 15%)
- Retail transactions +125% driven by payments/remittances, not speculation
- Corporate treasuries: MicroStrategy/Metaplanet 690K+ BTC = long-term holders, not speculators

**The paradox:**

- Infrastructure now scales independently (Stripe/Bridge, Visa settlement, Bitcoin ETFs)
- But: Most user activity still speculative (Solana "narrow range," memecoin dominance)
- And: Speculation survivorship "near zero" (Pump.fun 80% launch decline, NFT average $96)

**Verdict:** The casino bootstrapped the infrastructure. The infrastructure is now real. But most settlers are still gambling, not building.
