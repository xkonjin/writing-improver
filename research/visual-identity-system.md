## Prompt Engineering Template

**Formula:** `[Art movement/artist] style illustration of [concept], [specific visual characteristic], [mood/emotion], [color palette], [medium/texture]`

**Examples:**

- "Caravaggio baroque chiaroscuro of a Bitcoin mining facility, single dramatic light source illuminating circuit boards, institutional theater, warm earth tones and black shadows, oil painting"
- "Tsutomu Nihei megastructure brutalism showing stablecoin payment networks, repeating geometric precision interrupted by organic currency flows, monochromatic, industrial whites and rust"
- "Memphis design playful absurdity of AI training costs, clashing hot pink and electric teal, geometric shapes, joyful rebellion against financial rationality"

## Newsletter Visual Strategy

1. **Consistent visual system** — same 2-3 styles across all issues builds recognition
2. **Color constraint** — limit to 2-3 primary colors + accent; use negative space
3. **Header image dominance** — 60% of readers decide engagement from header visual alone
4. **Asymmetric visual weight** — 60/30/10 principle (dominant + secondary + accent)
5. **Match visual complexity to argument complexity** — weak insight = minimal (Bauhaus); strong insight = detailed (Darrow, Nihei)
